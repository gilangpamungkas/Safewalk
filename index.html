The Problem

Navigation apps ignore safety.
SafeWalk doesn't.

Existing apps like Google Maps route by speed — treating a dark alley the same as a busy high street. For millions walking London's streets alone at night, this is a real gap.

40%

Crimes go unreported

UK Crime Survey estimates fewer than half of street crimes are reported to police — yet even reported data reveals concentrated risk corridors.

1 in 3

Women feel unsafe walking at night

ONS data shows women significantly limit their walking behaviour after dark due to safety concerns — affecting mobility, health and independence.

3 APIs

Public data goes unused

Crime, collision and infrastructure data is all publicly available. SafeWalk fuses it into a real-time score — something no consumer app currently does.

How it works

From destination to
safer route in seconds.

SafeWalk fetches, scores and visualises route safety automatically — no manual input beyond your destination.

1

Set your destination

Search any address or tap a saved place. SafeWalk detects your current location automatically.

2

Compare routes

Up to 3 route alternatives are fetched and scored in parallel. Each appears colour-coded on the map.

3

Pick the safest

See crime count, collision history, and infrastructure quality for each route before you commit.

4

Walk with confidence

Arrival check-in countdown runs in the background. Your emergency contact is alerted if you don't check in.

5

Tap "I'm Safe"

One tap confirms your arrival. The countdown cancels and your walk is complete.

Features

Everything you need
for safer walking.

Built for real safety scenarios — not just navigation.

🗺️

Colour-coded route safety

Each segment of your route is individually scored and coloured — green for safe, red for avoid. See exactly where the risk lies.

🕐

Time-of-day risk adjustment

A route safe at noon scores differently at midnight. Crime patterns are factored by hour — up to 2.5× multiplier for late night walks.

⏱️

Arrival check-in

A countdown starts when your walk begins. If you don't tap "I'm Safe" in time, your emergency contact gets your GPS location via SMS automatically.

🆘

SOS button

Always visible during your walk. Tap to call 999. Long press to SMS your emergency contact with your live location link.

👥

Community reports

Report and see real-time hazards from other walkers — poor lighting, obstructions, antisocial behaviour and more. Stored in Firebase.

🏠

Saved places

Save up to 5 frequent destinations with custom emoji icons. One tap to set as destination — no typing needed.

Data Sources

Three independent
data layers. One score.

SafeWalk fuses open public datasets in real time — no proprietary data, no black box.

UK Police API Street crime by category within 200m of each route point ~50% (crime density) Free, no key
DfT STATS19 Pedestrian-involved road collisions — fatal weighted 3× ~30% (collision density) Free, open data
OpenStreetMap / Overpass Lit paths, pavements, crossings, surface quality per segment ~20% (infrastructure) Free, no key
Google Directions API Walking route alternatives with waypoints Routing only API key required
Firebase Firestore Community-submitted hazard reports Overlay layer Firebase project
Safety Score

What does the
score mean?

A single number 0–100, calculated fresh for every route. Higher is safer.

78Safe
52Moderate
31Risky

70–100 · SafeLow crime density, good infrastructure, few collisions

40–69 · ModerateSome risk factors present — consider alternatives

0–39 · RiskyHigh crime or collision history — avoid if possible

Time multiplierScore adjusts up to 2.5× for late night — same route, higher risk after dark
